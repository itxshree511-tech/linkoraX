Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LinkoraX

LinkoraX is a premium community membership platform that provides digital resources, learning materials, and a referral reward system for members who help grow the community.
The membership fee is 299 PKR. This is a one-time payment that gives you lifetime access to all platform resources and features.
When you refer someone who becomes a paid member, you receive a direct referral commission. Additionally, when your direct referrals refer others, you earn a 10% override commission on their earnings. The system has 2 referral levels maximum.
We accept JazzCash, Easypaisa, and PayFast for membership payments. For withdrawals, you can receive funds via JazzCash or Easypaisa.
You can request withdrawals once your wallet balance reaches at least 100 PKR. Withdrawals are processed via JazzCash or Easypaisa within 24 hours of approval.
We have 4 member levels based on referral count: Level 1 (0-19 referrals, 10% commission), Level 2 (20-49 referrals, 12% commission), Level 3 (50-99 referrals, 15% commission), and Level 4 (100+ referrals, 20% commission).
No, LinkoraX is not an investment scheme. It is a community membership platform that provides digital resources and a referral reward system. Membership fees are for access to platform features, not for investment purposes.
No, earnings are not guaranteed. Your earnings depend on your ability to refer new members who complete their membership payments. We do not promise or guarantee any specific level of income.
Membership fees are non-refundable. Once you have paid and accessed the platform, you are not entitled to a refund. Exceptions may be made for technical errors or unauthorized charges.
You can contact our support team by creating a support ticket in your dashboard or by emailing us at support@linkorax.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
